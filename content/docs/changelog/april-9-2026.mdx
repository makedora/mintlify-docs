## New features

### Monorepo project support

You can now organize your app within a monorepo. The mobile app runs from a subdirectory alongside your backend and other services, so you can manage everything in a single repository without conflicting configurations.

### AI docs analytics

Documentation pages now detect and track traffic from AI agents and crawlers. You get visibility into how AI tools are accessing your docs, including geographic data and user-agent details.
